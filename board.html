Board

The management of SCALES, in accordance with the COST rules, is based on the election of an Action Chair (AC) and Vice-Chair (AVC), and of a Management Committee (MC) that oversees all the activities of the Action. To facilitate flexibility in decision making within the network, a Core Group was elected in the first MC meeting. The Core Group encompasses the Action Chair and Vice-Chair, the Working Group leaders, and other key roles in the Action planning.
