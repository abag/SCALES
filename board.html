Board

The management of SCALES, in accordance with the COST rules, is based on the election of an Action Chair (AC) and Vice-Chair (AVC), and of a Management Committee (MC) that oversee all the activities of the Action. To facilitate the flexibility of decision making within the network, a Core Group was elected in the first MC meeting, that encompass the Action Chair and Vice-Chair, the Working Group Leaders, and other key roles in the Action planning.
